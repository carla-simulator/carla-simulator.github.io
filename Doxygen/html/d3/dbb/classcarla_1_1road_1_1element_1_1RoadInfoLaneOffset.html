Detailed Description

The lane offset record defines a lateral shift of the lane reference line(which is usually identical to the road reference line).

This may be used for an easy implementation of a (local)lateralshift of the lanes relative to the roadâ€™s referenceline. Especially the modeling of inner-city layouts or "2+1" cross-country road layouts can be facilitated considerably by this feature.
